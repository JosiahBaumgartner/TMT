Portland's SAT/ACT Boot Camp

Great SAT prep and ACT prep is a step towards accomplishing our student’s dreams. The so-called “elite SAT prep” may have sessions that last for months, that teach based on the average student. We know that there is no "average student". Our SAT/ACT Prep courses at TMT keep class sizes small in order to pay attention to the needs of each of our students. We concentrate our efforts on just the 4 weeks leading up to the test, because we know our students have better ambitions and goals to be working towards.

Students will dedicate one day a week to come in to take a practice test in the morning, and review during sessions in the afternoon. Through the 4 weeks of concentrated effort on recapturing concepts missed in school, and deepening each student’s understanding, we aim to achieve ideal scores in one shot.

Whether a middle school student looking for a good chemistry tutor, or a student with learning disabilities looking for a SAT subject tutor to get into an Ivy college, great quality tutoring should be available to students of all backgrounds. We at TMT will give our best, adapt and respect all to deliver the Portland Quality to all in Portland, Oregon.
